Local bookstore owners often still work with old sorting systems like the dewey decimal system. We believe that is outdated and both 
owners and consumers would benefit from an improved system. We plan on implementing a reccomendation feature that can take language based
user input return books based on author, genre, rating, and previously liked books. A user rating system that allows users to score books 
after reading them to influence future reccomendations, and a search/filter/sort feature that lets users quickly find books based on title,
author, genre, or rating.
